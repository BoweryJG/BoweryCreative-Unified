Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the resource library?

Our resource library includes 100+ marketing templates, campaign frameworks, content calendars, ROI calculators, patient journey maps, and exclusive industry reports. New resources are added weekly based on member requests and industry trends.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes! You can change your membership level at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing, while downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Are the workshops recorded?

All workshops are recorded and available in your member portal. Professional and Enterprise members get unlimited access to our full workshop archive with 50+ sessions.

What kind of AI tools do members get access to?

Members get early access to our proprietary AI tools including content generators, campaign optimizers, audience analyzers, and predictive analytics dashboards. Professional members also get beta access to new tools in development.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All membership levels include a 14-day free trial with full access to features. No credit card required to start your trial.
