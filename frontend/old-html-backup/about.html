Our Story

About the Founder

Hi, I’m the founder of Bowery Creative Agency. My journey has taken me from leading teams and launching breakthrough technologies in the aesthetics and medical device industries to collaborating with some of the most innovative minds in tech and healthcare. Most recently, I had the opportunity to work with Neocis—a company funded by NVIDIA and the creator of the first FDA-approved robot for dental implants. There, I gained invaluable experience in AI, automation, and the power of data to transform how we work and care for people.

I’m passionate about bringing together creativity, technology, and real-world expertise to help practices and businesses grow, adapt, and lead in a rapidly changing world. Welcome to Bowery—where innovation meets impact.
